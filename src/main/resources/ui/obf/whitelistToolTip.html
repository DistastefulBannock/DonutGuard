The whitelist is an easy-to-use setting that overrides
specific classes and resources from the blacklist.

Because both the DonutGuard black and white lists use regex,
you may find that this setting is of lesser significance
in comparison to other bytecode obfuscators.

The strings added to this list are compared to the paths
of different entries in the jar. This means that it uses
forward slashes(/) rather than dots(.) and includes file
extensions.

Clicking this button will open a regex helper site for
your use.
